For donors

Steps to follow:
1. If you are new to registration:
Click Sign Up > Fill details > Sign Up
2. If you have pre-registered:
Click Sign In > Email > Password > Sign In
3. You can update/delete your data.
4. On the home page there are two buttons one of them is search donors and other is donor login.
Click Donor Login > Fill details > Sign Up

For users

Steps to follow:
Click on Search donors > Select Blood group type > Select Pincode.
By going here you will get a list of people where they will have same selected blood group type and pincode. As a user you need to contact them in the ask of blood.
